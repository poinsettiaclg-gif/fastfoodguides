The "Medicine Ball" is arguably the most famous Starbucks secret menu item of all time. It became so popular that corporate eventually added it to the official POS system under the name "Honey Citrus Mint Tea."

However, just because it's on the menu doesn't mean your barista enjoys making it.

## Why The Kitchen Hates It
During a morning rush, sequencing is everything. Hot bar routines are designed around the espresso machine. The Medicine Ball requires the barista to completely break their routine. 
First, they have to physically open two different types of individually wrapped tea bags (which are notoriously frustrating to rip open quickly). Then, they have to run to the cold bar to get lemonade, steam that lemonade on the hot bar, and add honey. It completely halts the production line.

## The Pro Tip
If you are going to order this, try to do it inside the cafe rather than the drive-thru. Drive-thru timers are extremely strict, and ordering a Medicine Ball at the window will almost certainly tank the store's metrics for that half-hour block.
