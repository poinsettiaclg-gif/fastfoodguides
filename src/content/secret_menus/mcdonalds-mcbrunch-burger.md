The McBrunch Burger (sometimes called the 10:35 AM Burger) is a mythical creation that can only be ordered at the exact moment the kitchen transitions from the Breakfast menu to the Lunch menu. 

You take a standard Double Cheeseburger and stack a breakfast Round Egg and a Hash Brown directly inside the bun.

## Why The Kitchen Hates It
This is an absolute nightmare for the kitchen. Annoyance score: 10/10.

At 10:30 AM, the McDonald's kitchen is in "Changeover" mode. This is the most stressful 15 minutes of the day. The grill team is furiously cleaning the platens to switch from sausage temps to beef temps. The fry vat temperatures are being adjusted from hash browns to french fries. 

By ordering this crossover burger, you are demanding that they hold onto breakfast items (which are supposed to be thrown out or eaten by staff) while simultaneously rushing the very first batch of lunch beef. It breaks every rule of the changeover sequence.

## The Pro Tip
Do not order this at the drive-thru. If you absolutely must try it, walk into the lobby at 10:35 AM. Better yet, buy a McMuffin at 10:25 AM, wait ten minutes, buy a Double Cheeseburger, and assemble it yourself at the table. Do not make the kitchen do it for you.
